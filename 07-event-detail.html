About this event

Step into a retro-futuristic world at Neon Nights, the biggest Synthwave experience of the year. Featuring top-tier artists from around the globe, immersive light shows, and an atmosphere that transports you straight back to the glorious 80s with a modern twist.

Expect neon lasers, arcade corners, and a curated selection of themed cocktails. Whether you are a die-hard fan of the genre or just looking for an electrifying night out, this is the place to be.

  • Live performances by The Midnight and FM-84
  • Retro gaming zone featuring classic arcade cabinets
  • Best Dressed contest: 80s Cyberpunk theme
